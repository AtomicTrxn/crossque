# Crosscue

Crosscue is a clean, offline-first crossword app for solving your own puzzle
files on Android.

Import `.puz` or `.ipuz` crossword files, solve them with an interactive grid,
and keep your progress, history, and stats on your device.

## What You Can Do

- Import crossword puzzle files from your device
- Solve with a touch-friendly crossword grid
- Switch between Across and Down clues
- Check or reveal answers when you want help
- Resume puzzles with your progress restored
- Track solve history, streaks, and basic stats
- Use light, dark, or system theme

## Offline by Design

Crosscue is built around local puzzle files and local storage. Your imported
puzzles, solve progress, and stats stay on your device.

The app does not include built-in puzzle downloads yet. Future puzzle sources
will only be added when they are legally cleared for use.

## Supported Files

Crosscue currently supports:

- `.puz`
- `.ipuz`

## Current Platform

Crosscue is currently focused on Android.

## Status

Crosscue is actively in development. Core puzzle importing, solving,
persistence, archive, stats, settings, and onboarding are in place. The visual
design is being refined across the app.
